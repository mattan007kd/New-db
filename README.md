# SHLOMO // Finance Hub  ·  מרכז פיננסי

A private dashboard for all your money in one place — stocks, crypto, cash —
in the stone-and-amber ledger style.

## Open it
**https://mattan007kd.github.io/New-db/**
Access code: **LAPIDOT** (לפידות)

## How your data stays private
- The public page ships with **sample numbers only** — never your real holdings.
- To load your own: open **SETTINGS → IMPORT**, paste your portfolio, press **IMPORT**.
- Your real numbers then live **only in your own browser** (localStorage). They are
  never sent to any server. Clearing the browser clears them.

## How it works
- **SYNC PRICES** pulls live values for free — crypto (CoinGecko), USD/ILS
  (Frankfurter / ECB), US stocks (paste a free Finnhub key in Settings).
- Every number shows its **source and timestamp**. Sync successes and failures are
  both recorded in the Evidence Log. Mistakes stay visible.
- Two looks: **LEDGER** (stone & amber) and **HUD** (the original). Switch any time.

## Where it lives
This page is served by **GitHub Pages** from this repo's `index.html`.
To update it, change the file and it redeploys automatically.
